About Lens of Change

Lens of Change is a social impact platform that equips Northern Nigerian creatives with skills and resources to tell authentic stories. We combine training, production grants, and community screenings to strengthen the regional film ecosystem while preserving cultural heritage.

Train

Bootcamps, masterclasses, and a train-the-trainer model for scale.

Produce

Micro-grants and mentorship to create short films & documentaries.

Screen

Mobile cinema caravan bringing stories to schools and rural communities.

Archive

Digital platform with multilingual subtitles to reach global audiences.
